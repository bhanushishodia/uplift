About Us

Raising Standards in Elevator Maintenance.

UPLIFT is India’s first dedicated, technology-enabled platform focused on delivering structured, transparent, and reliable elevator maintenance services for low-rise residential and commercial buildings. Our solutions address a long-standing gap in the market where elevator care has been fragmented, unorganized, and reactive.

We offer a full suite of services including Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC), Routine Inspections, Breakdown Repairs, and Cabin Cleaning, with clear processes, professional technicians, and responsive support – all designed to minimize downtime, extend equipment life, and enhance user safety.

At UPLIFT, we combine industry expertise, process-driven operations, and digital convenience (including WhatsApp-based service management) to ensure that our clients receive proactive, hassle-free, and accountable service at every step.
